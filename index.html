What is Cyber Security

Cyber Security is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and personal data from digital attacks such as scams, malware, and hacking.

Why It Matters

Reasons Why It's Important!
Protects personal information Keeps your identity and passwords safe from hackers
Stops scams Helps you avoid falling for fake messages or emails
Prevents viruses Keeps your devices free from harmful softwares
Protects your money Stops people from stealing money through fake websites/emails/apps

Who Needs Cyber Security?

  • Everyone: because anyone can be a target.
  • Students: to stay safe on school Wi-Fi and social media.
  • Families: to protect their children's devices and accounts.
  • Workers: to secure work files and confidential company info.

How You Can Stay Safe

  • Use strong and unique passwords.
  • Turn on 2FA (two-factor authentication) for all your accounts.
  • Don’t click unknown links or download strange files.
  • Keep your software and apps updated.
  • Be cautious when using public Wi-Fi.

Glossary

Malware:
Bad software like viruses or spyware that harm your device.
Phishing:
Fake emails or messages that try to trick you into giving personal info.
2FA:
Two-Factor Authentication – extra step to secure your account.
